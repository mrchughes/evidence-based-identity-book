DCS Evidence-Based Identity Platform : Strategy & Vision

Created by Christian Hughes, last modified on Sept 18, 2025

Our strategic vision represents a fundamental paradigm shift in how government understands and processes citizen information. We propose transforming from fragmented evidence silos to a unified evidence-based inference engine that maintains the illusion of definitive decisions while managing probabilistic reality underneath. This platform would accumulate evidence across all DCS services, apply confidence-based reasoning to make appropriate award decisions, and enable sophisticated fraud detection through cross-product pattern recognition. By acknowledging that all government knowledge about citizens is inherently provisional, we create a foundation for citizen-centric services that work with reality rather than against it. The evidence-based approach would deliver measurable improvements in citizen experience, operational efficiency, and organizational capability while establishing DCS as a leader in intelligent government service delivery.
