Activity Diagrams

Citizen Portal User Journey

The citizen portal provides the primary digital interface for benefit applications, status checking, and evidence submission while leveraging the platform's evidence-based capabilities to streamline user experience through intelligent pre-population and evidence reuse.

The citizen portal journey emphasizes the platform's evidence reuse capabilities where returning citizens benefit from previously verified information through intelligent pre-population, dramatically reducing application completion time and citizen burden.

Caseworker Investigation Workflow

Caseworkers operate within a structured investigation framework that leverages the platform's evidence correlation and confidence scoring to focus human attention where it adds most value while maintaining comprehensive case management.

The caseworker workflow demonstrates the platform's sophisticated case routing and evidence presentation capabilities that enable efficient investigation while maintaining accountability through comprehensive documentation and approval workflows.

Policy Management and Rule Deployment

Policy teams manage the complex business rules that govern benefit calculations through structured workflows that ensure policy changes are properly tested, approved, and deployed without disrupting ongoing operations.

The policy management workflow ensures that business rule changes undergo appropriate testing and approval while maintaining system stability and citizen service continuity throughout the deployment process.

External Partner Integration Process

External partners follow structured onboarding and ongoing integration workflows that ensure evidence quality while accommodating diverse partner capabilities and security requirements.

The partner integration workflow demonstrates the platform's ability to accommodate diverse external organizations while maintaining evidence quality and security standards through graduated trust levels and continuous monitoring.

These activity diagrams illustrate the comprehensive operational workflows that the evidence-based identity platform enables, showing how citizens, caseworkers, policy teams, and external partners interact with the system while maintaining audit trails, quality controls, and appropriate security measures throughout all processes.
