DCS Evidence-Based Identity Platform : Success Metrics and Evaluation

Created by Christian Hughes on Sept 15, 2025

Quantitative Success Metrics

The prototype establishes baseline measurements and targets that demonstrate platform value while providing data for full-scale implementation planning. Metrics focus on areas where evidence-based processing should deliver clear improvements over current approaches.

Citizen Experience Metrics

Evidence Re-submission Reduction:

  • Baseline: 100% of prototype participants are new applicants providing evidence for first time

  • Target: 30% of participants who apply for additional services reuse previously verified evidence

  • Measurement: Automated tracking of evidence correlation across multiple applications

Application Processing Time:

  • Baseline: Current Manchester office average of 16 days for BasicSupport applications

  • Target: 8 days average with 50% of high-confidence applications processed within 3 days

  • Measurement: End-to-end time from application submission to decision notification

Citizen Satisfaction:

  • Baseline: Current satisfaction scores for BasicSupport application process

  • Target: 20% improvement in satisfaction scores for prototype participants

  • Measurement: Post-application surveys with control group comparison

Digital Channel Adoption:

  • Baseline: Current digital adoption rates for BasicSupport applications

  • Target: 85% of prototype participants complete applications through digital channels

  • Measurement: Channel usage analytics with accessibility compliance monitoring

Operational Efficiency Metrics

Caseworker Productivity:

  • Baseline: Current average case processing rate for participating caseworkers

  • Target: 25% improvement in case processing efficiency for evidence-rich applications

  • Measurement: Case completion analysis with complexity adjustment

Investigation Queue Management:

  • Baseline: Current investigation resolution times and success rates

  • Target: 40% reduction in investigation time for identity and evidence verification

  • Measurement: Queue analytics and resolution outcome tracking

Decision Quality:

  • Baseline: Current error rates and appeal frequencies for BasicSupport decisions

  • Target: 50% reduction in errors attributable to incomplete or misinterpreted evidence

  • Measurement: Appeal analysis and error classification with root cause identification

Technical Performance Metrics

System Performance:

  • Target: 95% system availability with 2-second average response time for citizen portal

  • Target: 90% of evidence processing completed within 10 minutes of upload

  • Measurement: Automated monitoring with performance analytics and user experience tracking

Confidence Scoring Accuracy:

  • Target: 85% confidence prediction accuracy validated through manual review

  • Target: 95% of high-confidence cases processed without requiring human intervention

  • Measurement: Confidence score validation through systematic manual verification

Qualitative Evaluation Framework

Participant Experience Assessment

Citizen Journey Analysis:

  • In-depth interviews with representative participants about application experience

  • Focus groups comparing prototype experience with previous DCS interactions

  • Journey mapping identifying pain points and improvement opportunities

  • Accessibility assessment ensuring inclusive design and service delivery

Caseworker Experience Evaluation:

  • Regular feedback sessions about tool effectiveness and workflow integration

  • Skills development assessment measuring confidence and competency growth

  • Job satisfaction impact analysis including stress reduction and role enhancement

  • Change management effectiveness evaluation through adoption behavior analysis

Stakeholder Feedback Collection

Senior Management Assessment:

  • Regular briefings on progress with strategic impact evaluation

  • Resource requirement analysis for full-scale implementation

  • Risk assessment updates based on prototype operational experience

  • ROI demonstration through quantified benefits and cost analysis

Partner Organization Feedback:

  • HMRC integration experience and data quality assessment

  • Local authority engagement opportunities and integration potential

  • External stakeholder perception of evidence-based approach

  • Cross-government replication potential evaluation
